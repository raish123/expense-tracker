Privacy Policy

We're committed to protecting your data. This policy explains exactly what we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

Last updated: May 10, 2026
01

Introduction

Spendly ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have regarding your information when you use Spendly.

By using Spendly, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

02

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account information: Your name, email address, and password when you register.
  • Expense data: Amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions you enter while using the app.
  • Usage data: Basic information about how you interact with the service, such as pages visited and actions taken.
03

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the Spendly service.
  • Authenticate your account and keep it secure.
  • Display your expense history and spending summaries.
  • Respond to support requests or questions you send us.
  • Comply with legal obligations.
04

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely and access is restricted to your account only. We implement industry-standard security measures — including password hashing and session management — to protect your information from unauthorised access, alteration, or disclosure.

However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

05

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information only in the following limited circumstances:

  • When required by law or in response to a valid legal request.
  • To protect the rights, property, or safety of Spendly, its users, or the public.
  • With service providers who assist us in operating the platform, under strict confidentiality agreements.
06

Cookies and Sessions

Spendly uses session cookies solely to keep you logged in during your visit. We do not use tracking cookies or third-party advertising cookies. You can configure your browser to refuse cookies, but this may prevent you from logging into your account.

07

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of any inaccurate information.
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at support@spendly.app.

08

Data Retention

We retain your account and expense data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we will remove your personal information from our systems within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.

09

Children's Privacy

Spendly is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with their information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

10

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of Spendly after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

11

Contact

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@spendly.app.
